How Surplus Food Connect Works

Three simple steps connect active restaurants and commercial vendors directly to rescue shelter volunteers.

01

Donor Posts

Restaurants and grocery storefronts list excess inventory, specifying quantity, expiry time window, and address.

02

Platform Tracks

Our interface displays dynamic listings, showing decay/freshness trackers, countdowns, and details to nearby NGOs.

03

NGO Collects

Shelters claim resources on our portal, coordinate logistics directly, and collect high-quality food immediately.
